## Saving your natural tooth

A root canal treats infection inside a tooth by removing damaged pulp, cleaning the canals, and sealing them. Your natural tooth stays in place.

## When you need one

Persistent toothache, sensitivity to hot/cold, swelling, or a darkened tooth are common signs. Don't wait — early treatment is simpler and more comfortable.

## What to expect

Many patients arrive anxious about pain. Under local anaesthesia, the procedure is straightforward. Most people feel immediate relief from the pain that brought them in.

## Recovery

Mild soreness for 2–3 days is normal. Avoid chewing on the treated side until your crown is fitted. Cost varies based on complexity. Book a consultation for a personalised quote.
