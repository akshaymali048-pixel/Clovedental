## Straightening your smile

Braces and clear aligners gently move your teeth into better alignment — improving appearance, bite, and long-term oral health.

## Who needs orthodontic care?

Consider treatment if you have crowded teeth, gaps, an overbite, or jaw discomfort. Early assessment for children (from age 7) can prevent bigger problems later.

## What to expect

Your first visit includes a scan and discussion of metal braces vs clear aligners. We explain how long treatment will take and what results you can realistically expect — no surprises.

## Living with braces or aligners

Aligners are removable for eating; braces need extra care when brushing. Cost varies based on complexity. Book a consultation for a personalised quote.
