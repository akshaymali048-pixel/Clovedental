## Restore your smile and confidence

Dentures replace missing teeth so you can eat, speak, and smile comfortably again. Modern materials look far more natural than dentures of the past.

## Who needs dentures?

Multiple missing teeth, teeth that can't be saved, or failing old dentures are all reasons to consider new ones. Implants can also stabilise dentures for a more secure fit.

## What to expect

We take precise impressions and involve you in choosing tooth shape and shade. A trial fitting ensures comfort before the final set is made.

## Caring for dentures

Remove and clean daily, soak overnight, and attend annual check-ups. Cost varies based on complexity. Book a consultation for a personalised quote.
