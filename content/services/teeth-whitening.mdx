## A brighter smile, safely

Professional whitening removes deep stains that toothpaste can't touch — coffee, tea, smoking, and ageing all take their toll on tooth colour.

## Who is it for?

If your teeth are healthy but discoloured, whitening can make a noticeable difference. We'll check for cavities or gum issues first — whitening works best on healthy teeth.

## What to expect

Treatment takes 45–60 minutes in-clinic, or we provide a custom take-home kit. Some temporary sensitivity is normal and fades within a day or two.

## Keeping results

Avoid staining foods for 48 hours after treatment. Cost varies based on the method chosen. Book a consultation for a personalised quote.
