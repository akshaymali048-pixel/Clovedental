## What are dental implants?

A dental implant is a titanium post placed in your jawbone that acts like an artificial tooth root. A custom crown is fitted on top, giving you a natural-looking, permanent replacement for a missing tooth.

## Who needs implants?

You may benefit if you have one or more missing teeth, struggle with loose dentures, or want a fixed solution that doesn't affect neighbouring teeth. Implants also help preserve jawbone structure after tooth loss.

## What to expect

Many patients worry about pain — most report only mild discomfort under local anaesthesia. The procedure is done in stages: implant placement, healing, then crown fitting. We explain each step before you commit.

## Aftercare

Stick to soft foods for a few days, keep the area clean, and attend follow-up visits. Cost varies based on complexity. Book a consultation for a personalised quote.
