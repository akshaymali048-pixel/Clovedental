## Dental care kids actually enjoy

Our kids' dentistry team creates a calm, fun environment where children learn that the dentist is nothing to fear.

## When to bring your child

First visits by age 1, regular check-ups every 6 months, and anytime you notice pain, discolouration, or thumb-sucking effects on teeth alignment.

## What to expect

We explain everything to your child in simple language before doing anything. Parents stay close by. Treatments are kept as short and gentle as possible.

## Building healthy habits

We teach brushing techniques suited to your child's age and recommend preventive treatments like fluoride and sealants when appropriate. Cost varies based on complexity. Book a consultation for a personalised quote.
