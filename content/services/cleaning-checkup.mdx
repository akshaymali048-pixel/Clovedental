## Prevention is the best treatment

Regular check-ups catch problems early — when they're small, painless, and inexpensive to fix.

## Why it matters

Even with good brushing, tartar builds up in hard-to-reach areas. A professional clean removes it and helps prevent gum disease, cavities, and bad breath.

## What to expect

Your appointment takes 30–45 minutes. We examine everything, take X-rays if needed, clean your teeth, and give you personalised tips for home care.

## No lecture, just care

We won't judge you for gaps between visits. Our job is to get you back on track comfortably. Cost varies based on complexity. Book a consultation for a personalised quote.
